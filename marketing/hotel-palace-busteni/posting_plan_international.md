## How To Position The Asset

Do not lead with:

- abandoned building
- old property
- ruin

Lead with:

- Former Casino & Spa Establishment, founded in 1907
- Approx. 7,700 sqm built area
- Approx. 4,300 sqm land plot
- Private property
- Redevelopment opportunity in Busteni, Prahova Valley
- Boutique hotel / wellness / clinic / events potential

## Best Asset Order For Listings

1. Teaser landscape video
2. Reel focused on overall identity
3. Exterior hero images
4. Aerial context images
5. A few controlled interior images
6. Long listing copy
